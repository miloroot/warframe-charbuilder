Kinds of physical damage.

Most of the weapons base damage is made from a combination of the three physical damage types: Impact, Puncture and Slash.
  • Impact:
    • -25% damage on Cloned Flesh enemies.
    • +25% damage on Machinery enemies.
  • Puncture:
    • +50% on Ferrite Armored enemies.
    • +15% on Alloy Armored enemies.
